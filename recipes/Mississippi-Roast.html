Mississippi Roast

Description

A sinfully delicious dish that is sure to be a hit at any meal!

Ingredients

  • 15 oz white corn
  • 15 oz cream corn
  • 1 stick butter (melted)
  • Jiffy corn muffin mix (1 box)
    • Â 
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 16 oz shredded swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Set the swiss cheese aside for the end.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all remaining ingredients together.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased casserole dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
  5. Top with shredded swiss and back for 15 more minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
