Mississippi Roast

Description

An easy but delectable meal that has the bonus of making your house smell delicious too! Goes great in-between brioche buns or next to mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 pounds ofchuck roast
  • 1 packet of ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet of au jus gravy mix
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 8-12 pepperonchini peppers (jarred)

Steps

  1. Place roast in a slow cooker and sprinkle the top with the ranch dressing mix and the au jus mix. Place peppers on top of the mixes, and add the butter.
  2. Cook over low heat for 8 hours.
